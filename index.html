This is Bethelihem

I began my educational journey at SOS Kindergarten and continued at SOS Hermann Gmeiner School, where I completed my schooling up to grade 12. My time there was filled with countless memories, from my first attempts to graduation. I remember the joy of playing, the camaraderie of helping prepare the table for our meals as part of our program, and the countless moments that still make me smile. Throughout elementary, high school, and preparatory school, I had many unforgettable memories. The teachers were incredibly supportive and would often ask me about my future college pursuits and my elder sibling's studies. They also made me feel comfortable enough to seek their advice on various matters, which was immensely helpful. In my first year at the Quiha Meles Campus of Mekelle University, it was a bit challenging as I had to manage studying on my own, my social interactions, how to behave with roommates next door and across the hall, how to manage my money, and with whom I should associate. At the Arid Campus of Mekelle University, things became easier as I gained experience and lessons from the Meles Campus. Now, I am still learning software engineering, and my journey continues.
